you have opened this website on a pc and have been given this page to explain how to get the app.

ios device:
to get started please open safari and go to freshcraft.play.ai:5001 then click on the 'share icon' and swipe down to add to homescreen and then a prompt box will pop up, click 'add' in the top right side of the screen (these steps may be different on different ios versions)

android device:
to get started please open chrome and go to freshcraft.play.ai:5001 then tap the 3 dots in the top right part of the screen. scroll down until you get to 'add to homescreen' and click it. then click 'add' (these steps may be different on different android versions)
